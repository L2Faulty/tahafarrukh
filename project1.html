AutoCAD Project – V8 Cylinder Head & Rocker Assembly

To further sharpen my mechanical design and CAD skills, I modeled a complete V8 cylinder head along with the rocker assembly using AutoCAD. This part represents the top end of a high-performance engine, where airflow, timing, and combustion efficiency are controlled.

The model included valves, valve springs, rockers, and a camshaft mechanism, each created with close attention to geometry and placement. I made sure the valve arrangement and flow paths for intake and exhaust were realistic, since these details directly influence power delivery, reliability, and long-term durability.

Working on this project not only improved my CAD expertise but also gave me a clearer understanding of how the cylinder head interacts with the rest of the engine. It highlighted why this component is often considered one of the most critical parts in engine design and performance.
