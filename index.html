About Me

The intersection of speed and engineering.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student, having just completed my first year. I've been passionate about automotive engineering and motorsports for as long as I can remember — especially Formula 1, which I started following closely back in 2015–2016 as a Red Bull Racing and Max Verstappen fan.

Over the years, that passion has expanded beyond F1 into endurance racing (like Le Mans) and MotoGP, where I've come to appreciate the complexity and innovation behind high-performance engineering.

Lately, I've also been diving into the world of aviation — particularly aircraft and fighter jet design and manufacturing. I'm fascinated by the engineering challenges humanity has overcome to achieve controlled flight and supersonic speed.

Currently, I'm building my skills through online courses in design, simulation, and programming, and I'm always eager to explore new tools and technologies that align with my interests. I'm an aspiring F1 engineer.
